About Us

Aedesol Technologies Pvt Ltd is an innovative IT and systems integration company offering a diverse range of services, including software solutions, electronic security (IP video surveillance, intrusion alarms, access control, and fire alarms), and home automation.

  • Software Solutions: Aedesol Technologies offers customized software solutions, including design, development, and implementation, to help businesses streamline their operations and improve efficiency
  • Electronic Security Solutions: The company provides comprehensive electronic security solutions, including IP video surveillance, intrusion alarms, access control, and fire alarms, to ensure the safety and security of individuals and businesses.
  • Home Automation: Aedesol Technologies offers smart home automation solutions, enabling homeowners to control and monitor their living spaces remotely, enhancing convenience, comfort, and energy efficiency.

Our expertise extends to electric vehicle solutions, kitchen automation, drone technology, and the Internet of Things (IoT), enabling smarter and more efficient operations for businesses and homes alike. Additionally, we specialize in custom software applications, mobile and web app development, and cutting-edge AI and robotics services, ensuring comprehensive and forward-thinking technology solutions. At Aedesol Technologies, we empower clients with secure, intelligent, and integrated technology designed to meet the demands of a dynamic market.
